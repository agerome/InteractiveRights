In or Out

How well do you know your country's constitution?

You can test your knowledge by selecting a country and playing Interactive Rights "In or Out".

  • Touch and hold a bubble
    to stop it's movement and get a better view of the Constitutaionl Right inside.

  • Drag the bubble to the bottom
    and drop it off in it's appropriate category, "In" or "Out".

  • Work together as a team
    to get the highest score possible within 3 minutes. Consecutive correct answers result in more points!

  • Tip:
    "The Circle of Rights" or "Raining Rights" can be of great help.

For further information on the data, feel free to visit www.constituteproject.org
