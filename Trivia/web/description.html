Trivia

In what ways has the United States' Constitution changed?

As the United States has changed over time, Amendments have been added (and removed) to the constitution. You can test your knowledge of the Amendments by playing Interactive Rights "Trivia".

  • Tap the screen
    to start the timer and get the first right.

  • Touch an Amendment Box
    that matches the description given.

  • Work together as a team
    to get the highest score possible within 3 minutes. Consecutive correct answers result in more points!

For further information on the data, feel free to visit www.constituteproject.org
